objective of the assignment

To enable students understand HTML and CSS programming languages in order to design a web site that considers a specific target group and is organized through the principles of user interface design and user experience design (UI/UX) to satisfy or address their identified target groups. Students can construct the content of their respective sites based on the understanding of their current and future plans for education, professional work and other interests that they would intentionally express through their respective sites targeting different parties (organization or individuals) that may be are in their immediate or otherwise environment but can support their education other goals .
